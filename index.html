OTCHO is a printed magazine for the fingerboard scene. Initiated in 2017 by Andrew Hill. First interviews were conducted in 2016, first issue released in 2017. The community is still young and underground, meaning there is little published information available. I conduct research by collecting oral history and experiences from members of the community as well as digitally documenting at fingerboard events. Due to the fast paced nature of the internet, much of fingerboarding history has been lost. Producing serialised, physical documentation of the community is key to preserving its background. After publishing 6 issues, OTCHO continues to share the untold history of fingerboarding.
